Humidity

There doesn't seem to be a strong correlation between latitute and humidity. However, as you get higher in altitude, the atmosphere becomes thinner (less pressure). This means that the total amount of water vapor the atmosphere can potentially hold is decreased. Further analysis can be done between altitude and humidity.
